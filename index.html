How to use this quiz

Keyboard shortcuts

A–ESelect an answer choice
EnterSubmit the current question (Tutor mode)
FFlag / unflag the current question
Jor↓Go to the next question
Kor↑Go to the previous question
HTurn the highlighter on or off
EscClose this guide, menus, and pop-ups

Question navigator (sidebar)

Open or close the navigator with the ☰ button at the top-left of the header.

Every question number is clickable — select one to jump straight to that question. They're grouped by section, with a progress bar, answered count, and flag count at the top.

Each number is color-coded by status — answered, flagged, and (once graded) correct or incorrect — with a matching legend at the bottom of the navigator.

The Reset quiz button at the bottom of the navigator clears your answers, flags, eliminations, and highlights so you can start fresh.

Highlighter

Turn the highlighter on with the marker button in the header (or press H). While it's on, select any text in a question stem to highlight it.

Click a highlight to remove it.

Strike through / eliminate answers

Right-click an answer choice (on a computer) or press and hold it (on a touch screen) to cross it out as eliminated. Repeat to bring it back.

Modes & tools

Tutor grades each question the moment you submit it and shows the explanation. Exam hides feedback until you press Submit & grade quiz at the end.

Turn on Grade (Tutor only) for a live running score, and use LOs to reveal learning objectives. (See the Question navigator section above for jumping between questions and resetting.)

Turn on Auto-submit answers (top of the page, Tutor mode) to reveal feedback the instant you pick a choice — no need to press Submit each time.
